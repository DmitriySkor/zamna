Transforming travel

Zamna’s Persistent Identity Layer is the critical foundation step in transforming industry data processes across a range of vertical markets including travel and financial services. By verifying persistent, assured identities between different data sources, airlines and OTAs let good, accurate data flow around all their systems.

This means they cut data management costs. They sell with confidence. They make decisions based on the right data. And they selectively share data with competitors and other third parties – all while ensuring passenger privacy and commercial security.

Zamna recently raised a $5 million seed round investment from Venture Capital firms including LocalGlobe, Oxford Capital, FOMCAP, CyLon and Seedcamp.

Zamna is an IATA One ID Strategic Partner, Disruptor of the Year Finalist @ the 2020 London Business Awards, UK Business Tech Awards Winner 2019 and holder of a coveted Oxford University Innovation Award.

Zamna`s mission is to solve the identity Black Hole while maintaining our three core principles:

We never STORE data

The data is matched only during the process of authorization of the customer in the network.

We never OPEN data

Privacy by design, GDPR compliant ( actually, enhancing)

We never CONTROL data

Act as a processor of fully anonymised data
